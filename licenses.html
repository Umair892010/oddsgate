Our Certifications:

The International Organization for Standardization (ISO) is a global non-governmental entity established in 1946 that sets quality standards for product creation and process management.

  • ISO 9001: Quality Management Systems

  • ISO 22301: Security and Resilience — Business continuity Management Systems

  • ISO 10002: Quality Management — Customer Satisfaction — Guidelines for complaints handling in organizations

  • ISO 27001: Information Security, Cybersecurity and Privacy Protection — Information Security Management Systems 

Gaming Laboratories International (GLI) provides the gaming industry’s leading testing and certification services, setting global gaming jurisdictions' most relevant technical standards. 

  • GLI-13: On-line Monitoring & Control Systems and Validation Systems in Casinos

  • GLI-19: Interactive Gaming Systems

  • GLI-33: Event Wagering Systems

The PCI Security Standards Council (PCI SSC) is a global forum that unites payments industry stakeholders to create and promote data security standards for safe global payments.

  • 3.2.1: Data Security Standard
